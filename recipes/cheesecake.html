Cheesecake

Description

A very easy and quick recipe to prepare a soft and tasty cheesecake that will blow your mind! All you are going to need are the following ingredients:

Ingredients

  • 300g of chocolate cookies
  • 50-100g of butter
  • 1 egg
  • 250g of cream cheese
  • A full tea cup of sugar

Steps

  1. Crush the chocolate cookies as much as possible, try to leave no solid pieces.
  2. Melt the butter until it's liquid and mix it with the crushed cookies.
  3. Mix it with a spoon so all the crushed cookies get mosited with the liquid butter.
  4. On a separate bowl, mix all the cream cheese with the egg and the full cup of sugar. Mix for 5 minutes.
  5. Place on a plate the chocolate base, and once it's settled pour the creamy mix on top of it.
  6. Hit on the oven at mid temperature from the bottom and top for 10 minutes each.
  7. Let it cool on the fridge for a while.
  8. Enjoy!
